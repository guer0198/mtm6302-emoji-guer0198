What is an emoji?

An emoji is a small digital image or icon that is used to express an emotion, idea, or concept in electronic communication. They are typically represented as colorful graphics or symbols and are commonly used in messaging apps, social media platforms, and other forms of digital communication. Emojis can represent a wide range of emotions, including happiness, sadness, anger, and surprise, as well as various objects, animals, and activities. They have become an integral part of online communication, helping people to convey tone, emotion, and meaning in their messages.
